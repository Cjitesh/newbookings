GODREJ HILLSIDE 3 - BY GODREJ PROPERTIES

Discover the epitome of luxury living at Godrej Hill Side 3, a prestigious residential project that seamlessly blends opulence and nature in the serene Hill District. Nestled on an expansive 2.7-acre land, this extraordinary development comprises two elegant towers, accommodating a total of 493 thoughtfully crafted units spread across 31 storeys.
